Product description

Size :None  |  Color Name:Red

The Coca-Cola 10 Can Retro Vending Cooler dispenses refreshing beverages at the push of a button. This unique vending machine features the classic, retro Coca-Cola design and can store and cool up to 10 standard cans at any given time. The tall display window lets you see whats inside and the push-button vending system lets you dispense drinks in a fun and creative way. Just push the button and reach into the clear plastic display window to retrieve your favorite drink.

The Coca-Cola beverage vending cooler uses quiet thermoelectric cooling, which cools up to 29F/16C below the ambient temperature.

For best results, keep the cooler away from any heat sources or direct sunlight. To ensure even air circulation, make sure nothing is blocking the fan at the back of the cooler. The cooler is best used in a temperature-controlled environment.

This cooler is ETL-certified to ensure unmatched quality and safety of the product.
